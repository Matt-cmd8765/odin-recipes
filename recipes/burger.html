How to Make a Burger

Instructions

This is how to make a good ol' fashioned American Cheeseburger! Before getting started please note this recipe is for making a burger on a grill. If you don't have a grill don't use this recipe

Ingredients

  • ground beef
  • burger rolls
  • salt & pepper
  • pickles
  • ketchup
  • mustard
  • AMERICAN cheese

Steps

  1. Make the ground beef into patties
  2. Add salt and pepper
  3. Let sit for 15 minutes
  4. While resting heat grill to medium-high
  5. Cook patties for 5 minutes on one side
  6. After flipping let patties sit for 2 more minutes and then add cheese
  7. Leave until melted
  8. Take patties off grill and put on buns
  9. Add equal amounts ketchup and mustard
  10. Add pickles
  11. Enjoy!
