How to Make a Pizza

Instructions

This is how to make a Pizza Pie

Ingredients

  • Pizza Sauce
  • dough
  • mozzerella cheese
  • pepperoni

Steps

  1. Let the dough warm up to room temperature
  2. Kneed the dough
  3. Make the pizza in a disc shape to the size of your choosing
  4. Spread out the pizza sauce
  5. Sprinkle on mozzerella cheese
  6. Add the pepperoni
  7. Cook at 450 degrees for 7 minutes
  8. Enjoy!
